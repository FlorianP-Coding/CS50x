sort1 uses: bubble sort

How do you know?: It is on average the slowest algorithm when the data is not sorted. However, it performs really well when the data is already sorted.

sort2 uses: merge sort

How do you know?: It is on average the fastest algorithm, no matter whether the data is sorted or not.

sort3 uses: selection sort

How do you know?: This algorithm is on average slower than sort2, but faster than sort1. However, when the data is already sorted it performs worst.
